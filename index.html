Spaghetti and Sausage

Spaghetti and Sausage is a simple dish that is easy to make, yet perfect for any ocassion. Date night, football game, dinner with your parents; This dish is bound to be a hit. It is elegant yet cheap, filling yet stress-free.

This dish is best made with spaghetti and italian sausage. Use marinara sauce and a bit of parmesan cheese to add a bit more flavor. If you're feeling adventurous, opt for the spicy italian sausage, to elevate this dish to the next level, and impress anyone who tries it.

Ingredients

*This is for one serving*

  • 1 cup Spaghetti
  • 2 Italian Sausages
  • 1 cup Marinara Sauce
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Fill a pot halfway with water and set it on a burner on hight heat to boil.
  2. Place desired amount of spaghetti into the pot.
  3. Place a pan onto a burner to heat up.
  4. Place desired number of sausages onto hot pan.
  5. Cook noodles for 7 minutes from the time the water starts to boil, or until cooked al dente (slightly soft but not mushy).
  6. Cook suasages until brown all the way through.
  7. Cut sausages inot bite sized pieces.
  8. fill a bowl with desired amount of noodles and sausage pieces.
  9. Place marinara sauce and parmesan cheese on top of noodles and sausage.
  10. Mix ingredients together to create a beautiful masterpiece of a dish.
  11. ENJOY!
