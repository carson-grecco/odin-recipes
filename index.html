Spaghetti and Sausage

Spaghetti and Sausage is a simple dish that is easy to make, yet perfect for any ocassion. Date night, football game, dinner with your parents; This dish is bound to be a hit. It is elegant yet cheap, filling yet stress-free.

This dish is best made with spaghetti and italian sausage. Use marinara sauce and a bit of parmesan cheese to add a bit more flavor. If you're feeling adventurous, opt for the spicy italian sausage, to elevate this dish to the next level, and impress anyone who tries it.

Ingredients

*This is for one serving*

  • 1 cup Spaghetti
  • 2 Italian Sausages
  • 1 cup Marinara Sauce
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
